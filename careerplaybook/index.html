#CareerPlaybook 023: Leaving On A High

WIP

01 Sep 2022 | in #careerplaybook

The typical life cycle of most things in life follow a bell curve. The exciting part is the way up to the peak. Which can include ups and downs.
But when the trend is going downwards on the bell curve, it is time to leave.

Bell curve here focused on the learning experience & personal growth.

Salary growth is only up and to the right but can also plateau without any significant step to be expected.

In Sales, you are only as good as your last quarter.

Just like TV shows that drag on for more seasons than they should have.

"You either die a hero or live long enough to see yourself becoming the villain"
