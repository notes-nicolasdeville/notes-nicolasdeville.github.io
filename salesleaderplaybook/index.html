Visualing the target and achievement

A daily visual reminder of where the team is at goes a long way

17 Jul 2022 | in #salesleaderplaybook tags: target quota

To drive constant momentum in a Sales team, I believe it's important to visualise, daily, where the team stands.

In my former office world, here is an example of what I liked to do:

Displaying on a wall the month's quota and past year's result - with an arrow that can be moved daily/weekly as deals get closed.

Obviously using a great month's result as example here :)

Note to self: review ways to duplicate this approach in a remote world (eg Slackbot).

Another approach is to celebrate the largest wins with a "Wall of Fame" - everyone (well, in Sales at least) wants to be on the Wall of Fame!

It doesn't have to be fancy to have the desired effect, as my example shows:
