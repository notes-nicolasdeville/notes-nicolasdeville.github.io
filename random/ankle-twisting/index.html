Twisted my ankle - one lesson

Go straight to the hospital

21 Jul 2022 | in random tags: health

Twisted my ankle 6 weeks ago.

I had plans to travel immediately after, which I did.

When I came back I booked an appointment at the GP - takes time.

She booked me for a MRT scan - takes time, and happened 4 weeks after injury. Got diagnosed with broken ligaments ("BÃ¤nder"), well actually just one, which isn't that bad.

Then needed to go to a specialist at the hospital - takes time, and happened 6 weeks after injury.

It appears the first 6 weeks of such an injury are critical for the healing process, which takes 12 weeks in total.

Lesson for next time: go to the hospital ASAP after the incident, even if you can walk and the pain is managable (for me A&E are for proper emergencies).
