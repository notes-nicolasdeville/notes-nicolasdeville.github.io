The EU's Digital Markets Act

10 Nov 2022 | in random

The EU seems hard at work regulating the Tech industry:
- the Digital Markets Act (DMA) will force Tech giants to make their platforms more open and interoperable in 2023. - the Digital Services Act (DSA) requires risk assessments of algorithms and disclosures about automated decision making, and could force social apps to open their data to outside scrutiny (implementation by 2024).

Source: Wired
