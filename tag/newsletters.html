Newsletter readers

ongoing search for managing email newsletters better.

16 Jul 2022 | in apps tags: newsletters

I have used Instapaper mostly over the last year to read my email newsletters (https://www.instapaper.com/).
Combination of high price ($25+/year) and awful layout rendering for newsletters (it's intended for webpages) made me search for an alternative.

SlickInbox

recommended to me on Reddit. Testing currently.
A unique email address is generated (+: can be chosen, so easy to remember) and needs to be used to (re)subscribe to all newsletters.

https://slickinbox.com/

Newsletterss

next try if SlickInbox doesn't work as expected.
https://newsletterss.com/

Meco

tried, did not work. Works only with Gmail - fetches newsletters automatically, in theory. https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1505542541
