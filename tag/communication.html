Producing Sales videos

because it's easy, effective and helps standing out

19 Jul 2022 | in b2b sales tags: communication video b2b sales

I have produced 150+ sales videos over the last 2 years, since starting to learn video editing. It has made a significant impact on how I sell & communicate.

Examples include:
- product demos (generic & industry or client-specific)
- walkthrough of client examples & showcases
- answers to RFP (gaining an edge vs pure written format from most competitors)
