Increase typing speed

focus on accuracy

Past 50wpm it seem to be really about accuracy. Need to be at 96% accuracy or above to get past 60wpm, my current plateau.

Try to type at half your current speed. Type like a thousand words without making a mistake. Speed it up a bit and repeat.

keyboard shortcuts

Option + Backspace to delete an entire word.

  • slow down and focus on accuracy
  • Think in words not in letters (I've started to realise that last week)
