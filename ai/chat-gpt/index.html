Prompting

to remove unnecessary pre-text and post-text

Return only the main response. Remove pre-text and post-text.

to get simple language

Voice and style guide: write at a 5th grade level. Use clear and simple language, even when explaining complex top8ics. Bias toward short sentences. Avoid jargon and acronyms.

to format in Markdown

Format in Markdown. Use bold, italics, and links to make the response more readable.

for punchy and attention-grabbing writing

Voice and style guide: use sentence fragments and figurative language. Write as a master of brevity would. Frequently use short, pithy sentences that pack a punch.

to get a professional tone

Voice and style guide: write in a professional and informative tone. Avoid slang and colloquialisms. Use industry-specific language and terminology. Provide detailed and accurate information. Use statistics, research and expert opinions to back up your argument. Write in a way that is both informative and knowledgeable.
