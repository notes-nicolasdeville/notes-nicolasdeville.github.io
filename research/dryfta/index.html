Contracting

Retainer

VP Sales activities:

  • Sales strategies (targets & ICP definitions)
  • Sales tactics
  • Setup of Outbound lead generation workflows & campaigns (multi-touch email & social)

Deliverables:
- target lists - documented strategies & tactics - campaigns setup - re-explore Twitter lead generation system - Proposal refresh & automation

The retainer aims to compensate for:

  • work time decorellated from revenue
  • lost deals due to company's inability to close
  • most importantly: benefits & revenues reaped from the strategies, tactics & campaigns beyond the term of the contract time.

Typical Base salary for a VP Sales in SaaS: $200k, ie $16.5k monthly [Nic Note: Compensation for Executives in B2B Tech Sales]

My typical retainer: $10k/month, ie $120k/year.

Retainer for Dryfta to be discussed, considering company's stage & finances.

Inbound leads

Why assign inbound lead to me?

Assigning inbound leads to me instead of dealing with them yourself will lead to:

  • higher close ratio, ie likedlihood to convert
  • higher average deal size

The commission structure is designed to ensure a win-win:

  • some "low hanging fruits" for me to get commissions
  • commission percentage results in net gain scenario guaranteed (Sold at list price, it's cheap enough to be worth it for the time saved by Zeb & focus on other things. Sold above list price, majority of net incremental revenue flows back to Dryfta, ie net net gain)
