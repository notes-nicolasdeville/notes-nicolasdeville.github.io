Ideas

Obviously hard to suggest ideas for GTM improvements without knowing the business, but here are a couple "out of the box" ones I thought of:

NewsWhip VIP as new Revenue Stream + Outbound Lead Generation

Assuming current main approach is "only" to engage brands, media & PR departments with a SaaS at $2k/month.

"NewsWhip VIP" would be a service for high net worth individuals (HNWI) and business leaders - packaging the NewsWhip SaaS with a dedicated "Watcher", at $4k/month.

VIP pays $4,000 per month to get a daily email tailored for her, surfacing what she needs to know to be successful along with potential PR threats to her/her company.

Daily email sent from an email address unique to the VIP so she can easily reply by email to ask her Watcher to make tweaks to her daily notification.

Staffed with the best social monitoring people, ideally with some domain expertise, eg Finance if targeting VIPs in that industry.

Provides the ground to engage VIPs with a tailor-made solution - outreach by email, DM on social media, etc...

The standard current SaaS offering becomes the fallback position, appearing as cost effective.

So if the VIP's response is

"Thanks, but I have already a team dealing with that for me"

the opening becomes

"Great, can you put me in touch with them? So I can explore how our solution would empower them to deliver you better results." (insert USP/better value prop)

Bottom line:

  1. new potential revenue stream at 2x current ARR
  2. new lead generation channel providing hot intros to relevant people with the leader's blessing.

Social Proof for Marketing & Inbound Lead Generation

Broadcast on social media highlights from the tools' output.

One account or channel per topic.

For example, @newswhip_renewable_energy would tweet x times per day the best performing media content on renewable energy.

Along with the trending indication - and teasing whatever else NewsWhip can provide as additional insights?

Duplicate by topic and across social media platforms.

Showcases what the product can do, while creating social proof, followership, brand awaress and lead generation.
