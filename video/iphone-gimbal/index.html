for stabilising videos

09 Jun 2024 | in video

A gimbal is a pivoted support that allows the rotation of an object on a single axis. In photography and videography, gimbals are used to stabilize cameras, helping to keep them steady while moving, which reduces shaking and ensures smooth, stable footage. They are popular in aerial photography using drones, as well as in action shots and filmmaking. Gimbals use motors and sensors to counteract unwanted movement, allowing for dynamic and fluid motion capture.
