Product

QiO’s Foresight suite of products delivers augmented analytics support to engineers/operators, and actionable insights and recommendations to senior management that make a measurable difference to business operational and financial performance of the business.

  • Foresight Optima: Reduces energy costs and carbon footprint, optimises renewable energy consumption, avoids tariff increases, allows plant managers to optimise production, quality, costs and energy without compromising safety.
  • Foresight Maintenance: Digital Health Record of critical assets, reduces maintenance costs, optimises uptime of critical equipment, increases asset reliability, increases capacity utilisation and safety management, and automates maintenance processes.
  • Foresight Service: Delivers seamless customer service support, supports prescriptive and predictive service requirement, warranty management, reduces total cost of service, accelerates timeline and access to information for service engineers.
  • Foresight Optima DC+ targeting energy savings for Data Center servers
