Thoughts

30 Jan 2023

Why is Tesla under so much fire from the media? A company pioneering new technlogies, disrupting an old industry that needed it, creating new jobs, and florishing ($4B quarterly profits with highest margins in the industry) - yet the media is constantly shining a negative light on it.

Why?

1) negative coverage gets more clicks (even the once-respected New York Times does A/B testing on their headlines I learned recently) 2) Tesla does not advertise, contrary to all other car manufacturers... who pay for ads in the media 3) Tesla is a threat to the oil & gas industry, which is a powerful lobby group that influences politics and media 4) Tesla's owner, Elon Musk, acquired Twitter because in the face of so much media misrepresentations, he wants to empower "citizen journalists". So he has become a target for the media.
