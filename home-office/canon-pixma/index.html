17 Apr 2023

Old Samsung laserjet seems to have died.

Ordered a cheaper inkjet printer, as our home office needs are quite limited re printing.

Canon PIXMA TS6351a

Canon PIXMA TS6351a Printer Colour Inkjet Multifunctional Device DIN A4 (Scanner, Copier, Photo Printer, OLED 4800 x 1200 dpi, USB, WLAN, AirPrint, 5 Inks, Duplex Print, 2 Paper Feeds), White
amazon.de
https://www.amazon.de/-/en/TS6351a-Printer-Multifunctional-Scanner-AirPrint/dp/B09RN1NTD5/ref=sr_1_3

Ink cartridges

Recommended ink:

PGI-580PGBK (PIGMENT BLACK)
CLI-581C (Cyan)
CLI-581M (Magenta)
CLI-581Y (Yellow)
CLI-581BK (Black)

  • a pain to setup
  • a bit flimsy
  • no ethernet
  • printing wirelessly via Airplay is a bit of a pain (need to turn on printer, then connect to it via wifi, then print) / vs. Wake On Lan with the Samsung laserjet
  • printing quality is OK but not great

You get what you pay for.

Next time I'll go back to a sturdy laser printer.
