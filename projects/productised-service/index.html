Productised Service

26 Jul 2023 | in projects

A productised service is a type of service that has been turned into a standardised and scalable offering, resembling a product.

It is an approach where service-based businesses package their expertise and deliver it in a more streamlined and consistent manner, similar to a product.

In a traditional service model, each engagement with a client may be customised, tailored to their specific needs, and priced accordingly. However, with a productised service, the service provider identifies a specific problem or need that many clients have in common and creates a predefined solution to address it.

This solution is then offered at a fixed price, with clearly defined deliverables and a standardised process for implementation.

Properties:

  • fixed price
  • fixed outcome
  • fixed deliverable
  • fixed scope
  • fixed timeline
