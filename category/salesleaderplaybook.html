2 types of successful SaaS Sales people

01 Jul 2022 | in #salesleaderplaybook tags: WIP
Updated: 04 Jul 2022

WIP

2 types of successful SaaS Sales people:

  • Product-focused
  • Relationship-focused

Relationship-focused are the traditional salespeople.

→ Best in commoditised Sales.

Product-focused are introverts, "geeks" about their product, experts who will help the client see the benefits, and how the solution can help them.

→ Best in premium/value-add Sales.

Greatest combine both.
