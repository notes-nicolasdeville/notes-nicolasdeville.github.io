Review

Probably the #1 book I'd recommend Sales reps in my team to read.

It's an easy read, more informal than most, yet clear on what selling means and how to do it.

Combined with a book like "The Qualified Sales Leader" The Qualified Sales Leader, by John McMahon or a Sales qualification book (like MEDDIC MEDDICC, by Andy Whyte) it covers most of what one needs to know about Sales execution.
