The Mom Test

Wish I had read it earlier! Highly recommend to all early-stage startup founders.

01 Jan 2020 | in books tags: startups

"The Mom Test", by Rob Fitzpatrick

Its full and clearer title is “The Mom Test: How to talk to customers & learn if your business is a good idea when everyone is lying to you”.

If you are a founder working through Customer Discovery, or even a Product Manager, I highly recommend “The Mom Test” by Rob Fitzpatrick which I just read.

Helped me identify some mistakes I am making, and gave me quite a few good actionable insights and questions to use.

It came out in 2013. Wish I had found it earlier!

You can find it here on Amazon:

The Mom Test: How to talk to customers & learn if your business is a good idea when everyone is lying to you eBook: Rob Fitzpatrick

The audiobook (available here on Audible) is read by the author, so makes it more “real”.
