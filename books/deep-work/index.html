I listened to the audiobook in 2019, and it crystallised a lot for me when it comes to work efficiency.

I love getting in a flow state, and since reading that book, I have managed to systemise my approach to create as much "deep work time" as I can - including:

  • blocking long chunks of time for deep work in my calendar to avoid booking meetings that disrupt work efficiency
  • removing distractions
  • music/playlists optimised to get me in flow
  • timer-based sessions
