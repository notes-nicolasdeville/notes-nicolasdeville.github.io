Compensation websites

Sorted alphabetically.

BIG4transparency

BIG4transparency
Get informed when it comes to your compensation.
https://www.big4transparency.com/

for Accountants and Consultants also! Total transparency and 9,200+ responses on salary

Blind

Blind
The #1 Tech Career Community
https://www.teamblind.com/

For Tech professionals.

A community discussion platform where people freely share their comp, they also have total compensation data aggregated from thousands of their users.

Builtin

Builtin
See how your salary stacks up
https://builtin.com/salaries

Comparably

Comparably
Compare Employers, Brands, and Salaries
https://www.comparably.com/

Originally for Tech, now expanded to all industries

You can compare salaries and benefits with others in your industry, and as a bonus, they do "Best Company For…" awards for categories like Diversity, Leadership, Women, and more.

Elpha

Elpha
Where women succeed at work together
https://elpha.com/

For women in tech and startups.

Over 8,500 reported salaries you can access. It's also a community of women supporting each other in their careers, with discussions on salary, career advice, and more.

Fishbowl

Fishbowl
Join others in your industry and company to get helpful advice from colleagues and coworkers.
https://www.fishbowlapp.com/

For professionals interested in large employers like Google, Deloitte, and Accenture.

Access salary 'bowls' for discussions on compensation, negotiations, and raises for your role/industry.

Glassdoor

Glassdoor
Millions of people are searching for jobs, salary information, company reviews, and interview questions.
https://www.glassdoor.com/

For job seekers across all industries.

Reviews are known to be biased at times, or even company-sponsored, but it's still a good place to get a sense of what it's like to work at a company.

PayScale

Payscale
Salary Comparison, Salary Survey, Search Wages
https://www.payscale.com/

Salary.com

Salary.com
Salary Calculator, Salary Comparison, Compensation Data
https://www.salary.com/

For anyone looking for salary information across different industries.

Provides salary data, a personal salary report, and has a cost of living calculator.

Levels.fyi

Levels.fyi
Helping people make better career decisions
https://www.levels.fyi/

For: mostly techies, especially software engineers

Detailed compensation data for different job levels, locations, and companies. They also highlight unique benefits a company has and assign a $ value to them.

OpenComp

OpenComp
Compensation Intelligence Tools
https://www.opencomp.com/

RepVue

RepVue
Sales Organization Ratings, Jobs, and Salary Data
https://www.repvue.com/

For: experienced sales reps & newbies looking for tech sales jobs

each company gets scored based on peer feedback, find out how much reps are making from $60K/yr. to top earners fetching $1M+/yr.

Rora

Rora
Salary Negotiation for Tech Professionals
https://www.teamrora.com/

Techpays

TechPays Europe
Tech Pays in Europe: but how much? You'll find out here.
https://techpays.eu/
