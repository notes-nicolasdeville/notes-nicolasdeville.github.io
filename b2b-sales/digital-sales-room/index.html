What is a Digital Sales Room (DSR)?

I have loved the idea of using a digital sales room (DSR) for a while now, though never implemented any.

Thinking of doing so in my next role.

I'm not sure at this stage if DSR is a common term, but basically referring to a digital platform that allows salespeople to share content with prospects/customers (and vice-versa).

This would include company information, product information, sales collateral, case studies, etc. all relevant to the prospect/customer and their vertical.
But also the contract paperwork, pricing, etc.

Similar to the "Go-Live Plan" in the MEDDICC framework, but more focused on the sales side ("Go-Live Plan" would be a section in this digital sales room).

I have referred to this in the past simply as "digital briefcase", as I'd want to avoid the term "Sales" in it. Still a negative connotation for many people in Europe.
